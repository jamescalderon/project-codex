About:

Project Codex helps businesses expand their digital footprint by creating simple digital products that help drive innovation and consumer engagement.

Mission:

Project Codex is a web design company driven by pioneering problem-solvers with backgrounds across countless industries. We exist to support businesses who have zero to limited experience with digital platforms dive into the digital space to service their customers.

Reinaldo Llano, Jr.

Reinaldo is a multi-lingual, tenacious, eager, loyal and super friendly individual who is passionate about working with businesses to integrate technologies in order to solve business problems and improve operations. He enjoys working on developing brands and making an impact in the community.

Reinaldo is a high achiever with more than ten years of experience in Media and Entertainment working for industry giant Time Warner Inc., and eleven years in Telecommunications working for regional industry leader Bright House Networks. He is a recognized expert in community relations, marketing and communications; program design and project management; employee engagement and volunteerism; social media audience development and engagement; technology training and curriculum development; and, in integrating finance and operations.

Reinaldo is a graduate of Hobart & William Smith Colleges (B.A. in Spanish and Hispanic Studies) and University of Miami (M.B.A.).
