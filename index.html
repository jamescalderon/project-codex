What is Project Codex?

Project Codex is a web design company driven by pioneering problem-solvers with backgrounds across countless industries.

We exist to help businesses dive into the digital space to better serve their customers. We help businesses expand their digital footprint by creating attractive and functional websites that help drive consumer engagement.

Our Story:

Project Codex is the brainchild of UCF alumni that met while studying web development. Our small but mighty team is comprised of developers that come from many different industries, each providing a unique strength and perspective to what we do for our clients.
